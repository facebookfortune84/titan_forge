# Affiliate Program Disclaimer

**Last Updated: February 2026**

## 1. Program Overview

The TitanForge Affiliate Program allows partners to earn commissions by referring customers to our platform. This document outlines the terms, disclaimers, and restrictions governing affiliate participation.

## 2. Affiliate Eligibility and Requirements

### 2.1 Eligibility
Affiliates must:
- Be at least 18 years old
- Have legal capacity to enter contracts
- Maintain an active email and website/platform
- Comply with all program terms and applicable laws
- Provide accurate information during registration

### 2.2 Disqualified Applicants
We may reject applications from:
- Individuals or entities with unsustainable marketing practices
- Those previously removed from affiliate programs
- Businesses engaging in illegal activities
- Those with poor compliance history
- Competitors of TitanForge

### 2.3 Program Termination
TitanForge may terminate affiliate participation:
- For violation of program terms
- For fraudulent or misleading practices
- For lack of activity (90+ days with zero referrals)
- For legal or compliance reasons
- At our sole discretion

## 3. Commission Structure

### 3.1 Commission Rates
- **Monthly Plans**: 20% recurring for first 12 months
- **Annual Plans**: 10% upfront, one-time payment
- **Minimum Commission**: $0 (no minimum threshold)
- **Maximum Commission**: Uncapped (no ceiling)

### 3.2 Commission Timing
- Commissions accrued upon subscription creation
- **Paid Monthly**: Commissions paid on the 15th of following month
- **Minimum Payout**: $25 (commissions below $25 held until threshold reached)
- **Payment Method**: Direct deposit, PayPal, or check

### 3.3 Commission Examples
- Referring 1 annual customer ($29,990): $2,999 commission (one-time)
- Referring 1 monthly customer ($2,999/mo): $600/month for 12 months
- Referring 10 monthly customers: $6,000/month recurring

## 4. Promotional Standards and Compliance

### 4.1 Prohibited Marketing Practices
Affiliates must NOT:
- Make false or misleading claims about TitanForge
- Guarantee specific results or ROI
- Impersonate TitanForge or employees
- Use trademark or logo without permission
- Engage in spam or unsolicited contact
- Buy branded keywords (e.g., "TitanForge," "titanforge.ai")
- Place ads on adult, gambling, or illegal content sites
- Use deceptive landing pages

### 4.2 Approved Marketing Channels
- Personal website/blog
- Email lists (opt-in only, not purchased lists)
- Social media (authentic followers only)
- Industry forums and communities (with permission)
- Legitimate advertising networks (Google Ads, Facebook Ads)
- Webinars and educational content
- Podcast sponsorships

### 4.3 Marketing Materials
- Approved materials available in affiliate dashboard
- Custom materials must be approved by marketing@titanforge.ai
- All materials must include affiliate disclaimer
- Testimonials must be authentic and verifiable
