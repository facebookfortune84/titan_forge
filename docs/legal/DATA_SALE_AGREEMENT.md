# Data Sale Agreement and Restrictions

**Last Updated: February 2026**

## 1. Policy on Data Sales

### 1.1 Core Policy
**TitanForge does NOT sell, trade, or monetize customer data.** Your data is your property and remains confidential and under your control.

### 1.2 What This Means
We do not:
- Sell customer data to third parties
- Use your data for targeted advertising
- License your data to data brokers
- Profit from aggregated customer information
- Share identifiable information with competitors

## 2. Data Aggregation and Analytics

TitanForge may use aggregated, anonymized data for:
- Service improvements and optimization
- Analytics and trend identification
- Performance benchmarking
- Research and development

This aggregated data cannot identify individual customers and is subject to strict anonymization standards.

## 3. EU and California Privacy Laws

### 3.1 GDPR Compliance (EU Users)
- You retain all rights to your personal data
- You can request data deletion or portability
- We process data only with your consent
- Data processors comply with GDPR Article 28

### 3.2 CCPA Compliance (California Residents)
- You have the right to know what data we collect
- You can request deletion of your personal information
- You can opt-out of data sales (which we don't conduct)
- We will not discriminate against you for exercising these rights

## 4. What Data We Collect and Protect

### 4.1 Customer Data We Protect
- Email addresses and contact information
- Company information and size
- Account usage patterns
- Configuration and settings
- Support communications
- Payment information (processed securely via Stripe)

### 4.2 Data We Never Sell
All of the above, plus:
- Personal identifiable information (PII)
- Financial information
- Employee records
- Business communications
- Intellectual property

## 5. Third-Party Data Sharing

We share your information only with:
- **Service Providers**: Payment processors, cloud hosting, email services
- **Legal Requirements**: When required by law or legal process
- **Business Transfers**: In case of merger or acquisition
- **Your Explicit Consent**: When you authorize sharing

All service providers have confidentiality agreements restricting data use.

## 6. Your Rights Over Your Data

You have the right to:
- **Access**: Request all data we hold about you
- **Correction**: Update inaccurate information
- **Deletion**: Request deletion of your data
- **Portability**: Receive your data in a portable format
- **Objection**: Object to certain processing
- **Withdraw Consent**: Revoke permission for data processing

To exercise these rights, contact: privacy@titanforge.ai

## 7. Data Retention and Deletion

### 7.1 Retention Policy
- **Active Account**: Data retained while your account is active
- **After Deletion**: 90-day grace period for recovery
- **After Grace Period**: Permanently deleted from production systems
- **Backups**: May persist in backups for up to 1 year

### 7.2 Deletion Process
1. Submit deletion request to privacy@titanforge.ai
2. Receive confirmation within 10 business days
3. Account placed in deletion queue (90-day grace period)
4. Automatic permanent deletion after grace period
5. Confirmation of deletion sent upon completion

## 8. International Data Transfers

For data transfers outside the EU:
- We use Standard Contractual Clauses (SCCs)
- We ensure adequate data protection
- You can request specific safeguards

## 9. Data Breaches and Security

### 9.1 Security Measures
- Encryption in transit (TLS/SSL)
- Encryption at rest (AES-256)
- Multi-factor authentication
- Regular security audits
- Penetration testing
- Access controls and logging

### 9.2 Breach Notification
If a data breach occurs:
- Affected users notified within 72 hours (GDPR requirement)
- Details of the breach provided
- Steps we're taking to remediate
- Available resources and support

## 10. Compliance with Laws

### 10.1 Legal Requests
We will:
- Provide notice to users of legal requests (where possible)
- Provide only the minimum required information
- Challenge overbroad requests
- Maintain transparency reports

### 10.2 Government Access
We do not provide backdoor access to customer data for government surveillance.

## 11. Employee and Contractor Access

### 11.1 Access Restrictions
- Employees access only data necessary for their role
- All access is logged and audited
- Confidentiality agreements are mandatory
- Access is revoked upon employment termination

### 11.2 Training
- All staff undergo data protection training
- Regular updates on privacy obligations
- Consequences for violations

## 12. Third-Party Audits

- Independent security audits conducted annually
- SOC 2 Type II compliance
- Results available upon request
- Penetration testing performed regularly

## 13. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy with 30 days' notice. Material changes will be communicated via email. Your continued use constitutes acceptance.

## 15. Glossary

- **Personal Data**: Information relating to an identified or identifiable natural person
- **Processing**: Any operation performed on data (collection, storage, use, deletion)
- **Data Controller**: Entity determining purposes and means of processing (TitanForge)
- **Data Processor**: Entity processing data on behalf of the controller
- **Subject**: Individual to whom personal data relates
