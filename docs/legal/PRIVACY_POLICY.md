# Privacy Policy

**Last Updated: February 2026**

## 1. Introduction

TitanForge ("Company," "we," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you visit our website and use our services.

## 2. Information We Collect

### 2.1 Information You Provide Directly
- **Account Information**: Name, email address, company name, phone number
- **Payment Information**: Processed securely through Stripe; we do not store full credit card details
- **Communications**: Messages, emails, and support tickets
- **Survey Data**: Feedback and responses to voluntary surveys

### 2.2 Information Collected Automatically
- **Usage Data**: IP address, browser type, pages visited, time spent, referral source
- **Device Data**: Device type, operating system, mobile network information
- **Cookies and Tracking**: Session identifiers and persistent cookies for enhanced functionality
- **Analytics**: Aggregated usage patterns and performance metrics

## 3. How We Use Your Information

We use collected information to:
- Provide, maintain, and improve our services
- Process transactions and send related confirmations
- Send promotional emails and service updates (with opt-out capability)
- Respond to customer inquiries and support requests
- Conduct analytics and optimize user experience
- Detect and prevent fraud or unauthorized access
- Comply with legal obligations

## 4. Information Sharing and Disclosure

### 4.1 Third-Party Service Providers
We share information with vendors who assist us:
- **Stripe**: Payment processing
- **AWS**: Cloud infrastructure and data hosting
- **SendGrid**: Email delivery
- **Analytics Providers**: Usage tracking and optimization

### 4.2 Legal Requirements
We may disclose information when required by law or to:
- Protect legal rights and safety
- Respond to lawful government requests
- Enforce our Terms of Service
- Prevent fraud or security breaches

### 4.3 Business Transfers
If TitanForge is acquired or merged, your information may be transferred as part of that transaction.

## 5. Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures:
- Encryption in transit (TLS/SSL) and at rest
- Regular security audits and penetration testing
- Access controls and authentication protocols
- Secure data centers with physical security measures
- Employee confidentiality agreements

**Note**: No security system is impenetrable. While we strive to protect your information, we cannot guarantee absolute security.

## 6. Data Retention

- **Active Account Data**: Retained as long as your account is active
- **Inactive Account Data**: Retained for 90 days after account deletion
- **Transaction Records**: Retained for 7 years for accounting purposes
- **Support Data**: Retained for 2 years

## 7. Your Privacy Rights

You have the right to:
- **Access**: Request a copy of your personal data
- **Correction**: Update inaccurate information
- **Deletion**: Request deletion of your data (subject to legal obligations)
- **Opt-Out**: Unsubscribe from marketing communications
- **Portability**: Request data in a portable format

To exercise these rights, contact us at privacy@titanforge.ai

## 8. GDPR and CCPA Compliance

### GDPR (EU Users)
We comply with the General Data Protection Regulation. You have rights to:
- Withdraw consent
- Lodge complaints with supervisory authorities
- Request restriction of processing

### CCPA (California Residents)
We comply with the California Consumer Privacy Act. You have rights to:
- Know what personal information is collected
- Know whether it's being sold or disclosed
- Request deletion

## 9. Cookies and Tracking Technologies

We use cookies for:
- Session management and authentication
- Preference storage (language, theme)
- Analytics and usage tracking

You can disable cookies in your browser settings, though some features may be unavailable.

## 10. Children's Privacy

Our services are not directed to children under 13. We do not knowingly collect information from children. If we become aware of such collection, we will delete it promptly.

## 11. International Data Transfer

Your information may be transferred to, stored in, and processed in countries other than your country of residence. By using our services, you consent to such transfers.

## 12. California Privacy Rights - Do Not Track

Some browsers include a "Do Not Track" feature. We do not currently respond to these signals, but we provide opt-out options through this Privacy Policy.
